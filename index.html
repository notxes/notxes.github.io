About Me

I am a recent graduate of Davenport University, a National Center of Academic Excellence in Cyber Defense Education (CAE-CDE) designated by the NSA and DHS, with a bachelor’s degree in Cyber Defense. I currently work in IT support, where I troubleshoot and maintain Windows and Linux systems in a production environment. My focus is on incident response and defensive security, with a strong interest in offensive security and adversary behavior. I actively build and maintain a hands-on home lab using Proxmox, OPNsense, Security Onion, and Active Directory, where I design segmented networks and simulate real-world attack scenarios. I take a practical, learn-by-doing approach by building, breaking, and securing environments that reflect real enterprise networks, with the goal of developing into a well-rounded SOC analyst.
